# JointVault v1 Product Requirements

## Mission
JointVault is a centralized knowledge and collaboration app for orthopedic sales reps to store, search, update, and share surgeon preferences, hospital information, product knowledge, notes, photos, and documents.

## Not in scope
- Scheduling
- Calendar integration
- Case management
- CRM
- Analytics for v1
- Notifications for v1

## Design
- Name: JointVault, renameable later
- Icon: hospital icon
- Style: business professional, clean, list-based
- Colors: navy and white
- Mobile-first with desktop sidebar
- Profile display: initials only

## Navigation
- Home
- Hospitals
- Surgeons
- Products
- Account

## Home Dashboard
- Global search
- Favorites list
- Recent updates
- Recent documents
- Recently viewed
- Quick actions: add note, upload document, add surgeon, add hospital note

## Hospitals
Hospital pages contain hospital-only information.
Tabs/sections:
- General
- Inventory
- Trays
- Contacts
- Photos
- Documents

## Surgeons
Surgeon pages contain clinical preference and technique information.
Tabs/sections:
- Overview
- Hip
- Knee
- Products
- Photos
- Documents
- Updates

Each hip/knee procedure includes:
- Implants used
- Trays to open
- Standby trays
- Step-by-step surgical technique
- Pearls and pitfalls
- Related documents
- Step-level document attachments

## Products
- Product notes
- PDFs
- Videos
- Quick-reference cards
- FAQs
- Pearls

## Documents
- Uploaded directly to JointVault
- Supported: PDFs, photos, videos, PowerPoints
- Multi-tagging: surgeon, hospital, product, procedure, keyword

## Collaboration
- Admin invites only
- Roles: Admin, Senior Rep, Rep, Read Only
- Changes go live immediately; no approval workflow
- Comments
- Version history
- Activity feed
- Draft mode

## Offline
- Users can pin/download surgeon pages, hospital pages, documents and photos for poor-reception hospitals.

## Extra approved features
- Custom fields
- Pinned pearls
- Procedure templates
- Global tags
- Archive mode
